Personal Brand
Designer
Syege
Syege was my outlet to create art. I learned how to fully cut and sew garments: pants, shirts, shorts, skirts, gloves, trench coats, backpacks. Every piece I designed and constructed from scratch. I also created shoe reworks, where I would take apart entire shoes like the popular Nike Air Force 1 and combine them to boots to create something new. I made hand sculpted silicone keychains, I made dolls, and for quite a while I have been on hiatus, but I will return and rebrand soon on a bigger scale.
